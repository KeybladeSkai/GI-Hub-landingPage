Webinar 1.0

A New Approach to Breaking into Technology

Exploring Opportunities in Health Tech

About the Webinar

Join us for an engaging webinar, "A New Approach to Breaking into Technology: Exploring Opportunities in Health Tech," where you'll discover the exciting intersection of healthcare and technology. This event is designed to provide actionable insights into Health Tech, covering computational biology, career opportunities, digital product creation, and the future trends shaping this innovative field. Whether you're looking to enter the industry or expand your knowledge, this webinar is your gateway to the future of healthcare.

  • Date: 11th October, 2024
  • Time: 4PM -6 PM WAT
  • Duration: 2 hours
  • WITH CERTIFICATE

Webinar Features: What you will learn and gain

Comprehensive Understanding: Grasp the concept of Health Tech, its various domains, and its significant impact on the healthcare industry.
AI & Computational Biology: Delve into the integration of AI and computational biology in healthcare, and how they are driving innovation
Digital Product Creation : Learn the process of creating digital health products and explore their transformative potential in healthcare..
Future Trends : Gain insights into the future of Health Tech, including the roles of AI and IT innovations in shaping the industry..
Salary Expectations & Leading Companies : Understand typical salary ranges in Health Tech careers and identify key players in the industry.
Career Opportunities : Discover the diverse career opportunities within Health Tech, along with the essential skills and certifications.

Why Attend?

Gain key insights into the rapidly growing Health Tech sector, understand how AI and computational biology are driving innovations, and learn about the skills and certifications needed to succeed. This webinar offers practical knowledge for both newcomers and seasoned professionals interested in transforming healthcare through technology.

Who Should Attend?

  • Tech professionals aspiring to enter the Health Tech industry.
  • Healthcare professionals looking to incorporate technology into their practice.
  • Entrepreneurs and innovators interested in developing digital health products.
  • Students and recent graduates exploring career paths in Health Tech.
  • Anyone curious about the future of healthcare and technology convergence.

What to Get?

  • Unlocking Health Tech : Explore the broad scope of Health Tech, from career paths to digital product development.
  • Innovating with AI and Computational Biology: Discover how AI and computational biology are reshaping healthcare and creating new opportunities.
  • Preparing for the Future: Learn about the future trends in Health Tech and the essential skills required to thrive in this evolving field.
