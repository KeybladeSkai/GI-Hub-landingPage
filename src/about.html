About GI-HUB

Innovative Impact

Genomac Innovation Hub (G-iHub) is a subsidiary of Genomac Holdings, focused on driving innovation, development, and commercialization across health tech and broader technological sectors.

Our Mission

Our mission is to be a powerhouse and leading platform of landmark achievements, transforming innovative ideas into groundbreaking products and services that improve lives and drive economic growth

Our Values

At Genomac Innovation Hub, our core values guide everything we do. We are driven by:

  • Innovation
  • Collaboration
  • integrity
  • Impact
  • Excellence

Our Interest

Our portfolio is designed to drive meaningful social and economic impact in :

  • health tech products
  • startup incubation
  • interdisciplinary research

How We Work

At G-iHub, we collaborate with partners and clients to foster creativity and develop groundbreaking ideas into market-ready products. We emphasize:

  • Innovation and Creativity: Generating novel solutions to real-world problems.
  • Collaboration and Partnership: Leveraging resources for maximum impact.
  • Prioritizing long-term, positive outcomes.
